Manage Datasets - Make PI owner of dataset

Need help?

As per SPARC rules, the PI on the SPARC award associated with the dataset must be made owner of the dataset on Pennsieve. This is because only the owner can submit a dataset for publishing and SPARC requires the PI to do so. Use this feature to give dataset ownership to your SPARC PI.

The SPARC PI must have a Pennsieve account and have access to the SPARC Consortium organization before you can give them ownership of the dataset. Click here for instructions on getting a Pennsieve account and access to the SPARC Consortium organization.
